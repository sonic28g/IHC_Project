KeyVault Games


This an idea of ​​an online platform designed to be a one-stop shop for gamers to purchase digital game keys with interactive functions. Our goal is to have a platform for both casual and hardcore gamers to use and decide which games to buy, or not.

A game key is a unique code used to activate and gain access to a digital video game. When you enter the game key into a digital distribution platform, such as Steam, Playstation Store or the Epic Games Store, it verifies your purchase and allows you to download, install, and play the game.
